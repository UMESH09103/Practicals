Introduction

Ant Colony Optimization is a probabilistic technique used to solve optimization problems like TSP by simulating ant behavior.

Working
Initialize pheromone levels
Each ant constructs a route
Probability depends on pheromone and distance
Evaluate route length
Update pheromone trails
Repeat for multiple iterations
Observation
Distance decreases over iterations
Algorithm converges to shortest path
Conclusion

ACO effectively finds near-optimal solutions for TSP using collective intelligence.
